DRAGON NEST
❄️ ICEMOTHER’S VIGIL ❄️

Step into the scales of a mighty ice dragon and guard the one treasure that matters most—your egg. In this immersive VR fantasy, you are no rider or summoner, but the dragon itself. Unleash breathtaking ice magic, sweep enemies aside with your wings, and roar storms into being.

Goblin tribes, lizardfolk, wyverns, gargoyles, and even frost giants seek to steal your egg. Waves of enemies will descend upon your lair in four relentless rounds. Only your strength and cunning will keep them at bay. Survive all 4 rounds, and your egg will hatch in safety. Fail, and its light will be chained, corrupted, and lost to the enemy.
